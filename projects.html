Windows 11 STIG Compliance

As part of a comprehensive cybersecurity initiative, I conducted a detailed security audit on a company's Windows 11 network infrastructure, evaluating compliance against Microsoft's Security Technical Implementation Guides (STIG) SCAP Benchmark. My role involved scanning systems for vulnerabilities, analyzing compliance gaps, and prioritizing risks through a structured risk register.

Key challenges included addressing critical issues such as unencrypted drives, weak authentication protocols, and insecure remote access configurations, and effectively communicating these technical risks to non-technical stakeholders.

Based on the findings of this assessment, I collaborated with a team to develop a tailored Corporate Security Policy aligned with the Center for Internet Security (CIS) Critical Security Controls framework. The policy addressed areas such as remote access, VPN usage, data protection, and incident response, with particular emphasis on securing cloud services and remote connections essential to the company's daily operations.

This project strengthened my skills in technical auditing, risk assessment, policy development, and cross-functional communication. The outcome was a clear, actionable security improvement plan and corporate policy framework that significantly enhanced the organization's security posture while supporting business continuity.
