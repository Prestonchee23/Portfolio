Windows 11 STIG Compliance

Audited Windows 11 systems using STIG SCAP Benchmarks...

Overview:

As part of a comprehensive cybersecurity initiative, I conducted a detailed security audit of a company’s Windows 11 network infrastructure. My focus was on evaluating compliance with Microsoft’s Security Technical Implementation Guides (STIG) using SCAP Benchmark tools. I scanned systems for vulnerabilities, analyzed compliance gaps, and prioritized risks through a structured risk register.

Following the audit, I collaborated with a cross-functional team to develop a tailored Corporate Security Policy aligned with the Center for Internet Security (CIS) Critical Security Controls framework. The policy addressed remote access, VPN usage, data protection, and incident response, with a strong emphasis on securing cloud services and remote connections vital to daily operations.

Complex Challenges Faced:

  • Identifying and remediating critical issues such as unencrypted drives and weak authentication protocols
  • Addressing insecure remote access configurations across distributed systems
  • Communicating technical risks to non-technical stakeholders in a clear, actionable way
  • Aligning policy development with both compliance standards and operational needs
  • Ensuring cloud services met security requirements without disrupting workflows

Ethical Considerations and Actions Taken:

  • Maintained strict confidentiality of audit findings and system vulnerabilities
  • Ensured all recommendations adhered to legal and organizational compliance standards
  • Promoted transparency in risk prioritization and remediation planning
  • Advocated for user education and secure practices without placing blame

Outcomes:

  • Delivered a clear, actionable security improvement plan and policy framework
  • Enhanced the organization’s overall security posture and business continuity readiness
  • Strengthened skills in technical auditing, risk assessment, and policy development
  • Improved cross-functional communication between technical and non-technical teams
